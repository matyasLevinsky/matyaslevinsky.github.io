```
description: "Analysis of Czech municipal tax revenue distribution with proposals for reform to better support regional development."
categories: [publication, second author, literature review]

This study looks at how tax revenues are distributed to Czech municipalities and identifies five structural problems with the current system — from underfunded regional centers to weak incentives for inter-municipal cooperation. description: "Analysis of Czech municipal elections revealing the near-disappearance of left-wing parties and widespread uncontested voting."
```
```
categories: [publication, author, data analysis]
---

A deep dive into Czech municipal elections revealing that nearly a third of municipalities run uncontested — with over 700,000 citizens having no real electoral choice. We also document the near-complete collapse of left-wing parties at the local level since 2002. This was an interesting project for me as it combined electoral data with demographic and geographic context.
```

```
date: 2024-04-04
description: "Benchmarking Czech philanthropy against Europe and proposing tax reforms to encourage charitable giving."
categories: [publication, second author, literature review]
---

This study benchmarks Czech philanthropic giving against other European countries and finds that while donation levels are comparable to Austria or Slovakia, the tax system does relatively little to encourage giving — especially for middle-income donors. We draw on models from Denmark and the Netherlands to propose reforms. I worked on the comparative data collection and cross-country analysis.
```

## rozdrobene-obce.qmd

```
---
title: "Rozdrobené obce, drahé služby, přetížení starostové. Řešením je reforma společenství obcí"
date: 2026-02-25
description: "Proposal to strengthen municipal associations in the Czech Republic, modeled on France's intercommunal cooperation system."
categories: [publication, author, literature review]
---

Most Czech municipalities have fewer than 500 residents and struggle to deliver even basic services on their own. In this study we argue that strengthening municipal associations — modeled on the French intercommunal system — would allow small municipalities to pool resources and responsibilities while preserving local democratic accountability. I contributed to the data analysis and empirical background for the reform proposal.

[Read the original study →](https://www.paqresearch.cz/post/rozdrobene-obce-drahe-sluzby-pretizeni-starostove-resenim-je-reforma-spolecenstvi-obci/)
```
